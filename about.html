THE STORY OF A PHOTOGRAPHER

Hey! I am Manoj. I fell in love with photographing for over 7 years ago. Started taking selfies, natures, and moved onto portraits and commercial photographing. One of the biggest motivations is I am free to play around with lighting, colors and composition. After all, there is no two things alike. Manual settings on camera and natural light are always my priorities. It is more creativity challenging and requires new perspectives and angles. Countless moments happen every second, and my goal is to capture those moments throughout my creative journey. From concept to styling, step by step.
